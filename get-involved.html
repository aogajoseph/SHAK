HOW CAN YOU HELP?

At SHAK Nairobi, collective action amplifies the strength for change and a better future. Your support is a huge driver in changing the lives of people living with Spina bifida and Hydrocephalus. Get involved now and make a lasting impact!

SHAK NAIROBI CAUSES

PRIMARY PREVENTION

We distribute informative materials and folic acid supplements to women of child bearing ages through the House of Hope project. With your help, we can reach more women.

ADVOCACY & PUBLICITY

We advocate for disability rights on inclusion and mainstreaming and publicise awareness on Spina bifida and Hydrocephalus. Your support fuels these efforts.

LIFELONG CARE

We accommodate, feed, train and rehabilitate children with Spina bifida and Hydrocephalus and their caregivers through the House of Hope project as they seek treatment.
