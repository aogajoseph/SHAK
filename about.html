About us

Who We Are

SPINABIFIDA and HYDROCEPHALUS ASSOCIATION of Kenya, SHAK Nairobi is a lobby and advocacy organization that creates awareness and sensitization on disability inclusion and mainstreaming of persons with Spina bifida and Hydrocephalus.

Mission

To provide hope, care and healing to persons living with Spina bifida and Hydrocephalus..

Vision

Improving the quality of life for persons living with Spina bifida and Hydrocephalus through:
  • Primary prevention (good nutrition, food fortification, and supplementation)
  • Timely detection and early intervention.
  • Lifelong care and rehabilitation
  • Capacity building for parents, gurdians and caregivers.
  • Advocacy, lobbying and inclusion.

Our Story

Juliana Auma the founder of SHAK Nairobi, was a high school student in Nairobi in the '90s. She got pregnant with Phoebe who was born with Spina Bifida and Hydrocephalus forcing her to drop her education to attend to Phoebe's special needs.
