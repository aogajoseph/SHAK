ABOUT US

Who We Are

SPINABIFIDA and HYDROCEPHALUS ASSOCIATION of Kenya, SHAK - Nairobi is a Community Based Organization registered in 2013 under the Societies Act Cap 108 of the Laws of Kenya to promote health and wellness of persons with Spina bifida and Hydrocephalus through Publicity, Lifelong care and Prevention. The Organization was founded by a group of parents who felt the urge to come together and support one another in caring for children with Spina bifida and Hydrocephalus.

Mission

"To provide hope, care and healing to persons living with Spina bifida and Hydrocephalus, their families and caregivers."

Vision

"To help persons with Spina bifida and Hydrocephalus live an improved life."

OUR STORY

SHAK Nairobi was founded in 2010 by parents of children with Spina bifida and Hydrocephalus. After experiencing more or less similar challenges, they felt the urge to come together and support one another in the journey of bringing up their children and caring for them.
