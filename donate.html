HOW CAN YOU HELP?

Donate to SHAK Causes

At SHAK, the strength for change is amplified by collective action. We invite you to take part in making the difference with us. Your support is a huge driver in changing the lives of people living with Spina bifida and Hydrocephalus. Donate now and make a lasting impact.

CAUSES

PRIMARY PREVENTION

We distribute informative materials and folic acid supplements to women of child bearing ages through the House of Hope project. With your help, we can reach more women.

ADVOCACY

We push for disability rights on inclusion and mainstreaming while publicising and promoting awareness on Spina bifida and Hydrocephalus. Your support fuels these efforts.

LIFELONG CARE

We accommodate, feed, train and rehabilitate children with Spina bifida and Hydrocephalus and their caregivers through the House of Hope project as they seek treatment.
