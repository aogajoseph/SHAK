HOUSE OF HOPE NAIROBI

House of Hope Nairobi is a "halfway home" for persons with Spina bifida and Hydrocephalus seeking treatment in hospitals accross Nairobi. This initiative provides free meals, accommodation, training on Spina bifida and Hydrocephalus, basic rehabilitation and empowerment programs for parents and children.

It was established by SHAK Nairobi in 2014 in Kijabe, Kiambu county. Before the House of Hope project was established, parents and gurdians of people with Spina bifida and Hydrocephalus needed a place to stay as they sought medical care at Bethany Kids and Kijabe hospital.
